Career

Cristiano Ronaldo started his professional career at Sporting CP before moving to Manchester United, where he won his first Ballon d'Or. He then had legendary stints with Real Madrid and Juventus.

Achievement

Ronaldo has won 5 Ballon d'Or awards, 4 Golden Shoes, 7 league titles, and 5 Champions League trophies, among numerous other honors.

Legacy

Ronaldo is considered one of the greatest footballers of all time, having broken numerous records and inspired millions globally with his hard work, discipline, and unmatched skill on the field.
